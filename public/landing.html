04 · Questions

Everything you need before you switch.

How quickly can we go live? +
Setup time depends on your data readiness. The project includes pages and APIs for settings, employee records, and day-to-day operations — most teams are operational within a day.
Can this work for multi-location teams? +
Yes. Karigar supports distributed teams with centralised oversight and role-aware access for each location manager. Admins retain full visibility across all sites.
Will this reduce payroll disputes? +
The platform centralises attendance, credits, and task records into one auditable source of truth — significantly reducing discrepancies when it's time to run payroll.
Do we need technical staff to operate it? +
Day-to-day use is product-led and requires no technical knowledge. Initial deployment and infra management still require standard web app operations support.
Is our data secure? +
Absolutely. All write operations use CSRF validation, rate limiting, and input sanitization. Auth is session-based with HTTP-only cookies and role-based access control.
